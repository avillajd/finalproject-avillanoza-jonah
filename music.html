I have been producing music under the name Ninety-Two.These songs are a collection of a couple things I’ve released on all streaming platforms. I believe as a producer I wanted to provide a sound to people I thought was unique and most importantly was cool to me. I’m a 90’s kid, a lot of the sounds you hear should be reminiscent of growing up in that time period with a touch of progression. You’ll hear video game sounds, nature, and 808’s most of the time. I try to create music with a vibe and steer away from being put in a box as far as genre goes. I grew up listening to all music from 3-6 Mafia to Sade and beyond!


I’m currently recording 4 artists on a regular basis and am proficient in Ableton Live 11. I am still learning Pro Tools. I enjoy playing guitar, drums, Moog Synthesizers and experimenting in sound design to bring my music to life. Let’s Work!
