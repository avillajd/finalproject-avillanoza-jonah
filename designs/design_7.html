Rigged For Sea. | "Drifting"

2021 | Virginia Beach.

Shirt Design.

Africa Deployment Series.

While I was deployed in Africa there were many lulls in times and operations. At the time I was doing a lot of black and white line work. I was exploring more into the tattoo style of illustration. The idea was to make something extremely beautiful and to give it a touch of mystery and wonder. The graphic displays a beautiful woman that one could infer to be a drifting personality.
