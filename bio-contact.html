I look for things that challenge me in my creative process. I like to focus on content that I enjoy for my self. There is heavy use of gesture in my work. There is plenty time for searching and calling for patience. Professional work has a pace and Fine Art has a pace. Both are always on time.


If you are not having FUN creating work you should stop. I believe art should still be art. People lose sight of that sometimes and try to make it something it’s not. It means something different to everybody. I am currently having a goodtime, so I’ll continue to create. Mucha, Moser and Roller forever.


-Avillanoza
